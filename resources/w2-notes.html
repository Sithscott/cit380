Project Management

CIT 380

Week 2 Teaching Notes

Outcomes

By the end of the lesson, each student will:

  1. Carry out a simple project
  2. Explain and evaluate project ideas

Before the Lesson Begins

  1. Review the Meal project to be able to anticipate questions that might arise.

During the Lesson

  1. This week you should give lots of coaching about how to help with the project selection process. Open the discussion board after it is graded.
  2. Point out the 3 plans. If a student is struggling, invite him/her to spend some time reviewing Plan C regardless what they end up doing.
  3. Inform students of your office hours.
  4. Answer student questions via email, office hours, and Announcements discussion.
