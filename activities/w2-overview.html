Project Management

CIT 380

Week 2 Overview

Outcomes

By the end of the lesson, each student will:

  1. Carry out a simple project.
  2. Explain and evaluate project ideas.
  3. Choose a path.

Overview

This week will give you the opportunity to learn about some of the different project management methodologies, and then plan a simple project using one of them. You will also need to come up with a project idea of your own and try to entice classmates to join your project.

You should also review your classmates ideas...maybe one of them will have an idea that appeals to you more than your own does!

Prepare

  • Carefully read the Syllabus
  • Read as needed in the PMBOK Guide.
  • Review the glossary at the back of the PMBOK Guide
  • Think about a project idea you would be interested in completing.

Ponder

  • Create an elevator pitch for your project idea, and post this in the weekly discussion board.
  • Review your classmate's pitches. Respond to any that are interesting to you. Post any questions you have for them and state your interest.
  • Continue thinking about which path you are interested in following to complete this course. You should really make this decision by the end of this week. The paths are outlined in the syllabus. And more details can be found here: Challenge path, Semi-structured path, and Highly structured path.

Prove

  • Complete the Meal project
