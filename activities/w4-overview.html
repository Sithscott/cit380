Week 4 Overview

Outcomes

By the end of the semester, each student will:

  1. Be an effective communicator, team member, and leader
  2. Be proactive, able to operate in poorly defined or constrained situations
  3. Know how to create, coordinate, execute, and evaluate a project plan
  4. Be self-regulated, life-long learners

Overview

No matter which plan you are on, you whould be working on your project.

Prepare

  • Carefully review your schedule and communications plans to make sure that everyone knows what to do this week to progress the project.

Ponder

  • Plan your time to make sure that you will accomplish all of the tasks required for this week. Communicate with your team if you have one what you will be working on and your progress.
