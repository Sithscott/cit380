Project Management

CIT 380

Week 3 Overview

Outcomes

By the end of the lesson, each student will:

  1. Challenge and Semi-structured Plans:
    1. Negotiate a project with a customer/sponsor
    2. Create a project charter
    3. Effectively communicate project ideas with peers and other potential
    4. Evaluate project proposals
  2. Structured Plan: Begin following the provided Project Plan.

Overview

By the beginning of this week you should have selected a plan that you will begin following. If you choose the Structured Plan (Plan 3) You should begin by reviewing the plan details and begin executing it.

If you have chosen the Challenge (Plan 1) or Semi-structured (2) plans you need to assess if you are more interested in your project idea or someone elses. If you decide to pursue your idea then review the students who expressed interest in your plan. Follow up with them to build your team (if the project is a team project). Then with your team you should prepare a Charter and a Project Management plan.

Prepare

  • Carefully read the Syllabus
  • Read as needed in the PMBOK Guide.
  • Plan 1 and 2: Review the projects discussion from the previous week. Decide whether you will pursue building a team for your project idea, or pursue joining someone elses. Follow up in either case. Do this before mid week!
  • Plan 3: Become familiar with the project details. Structured Plan (3)

Ponder

  • Plans 1 and 2: Choose a project management methodology that you would like to learn more about, and that you think will work well with your project choice. Become familiar with the methodology and the details for your plan. Challenge Plan (1), Semi structured Plan (2)
  • Plan 3: Begin your project.

Prove

  • Plan 1 and 2: Create a charter and Project Management plan for your project.
